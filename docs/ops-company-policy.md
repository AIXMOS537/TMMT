# TMMT / AIXMOS — Company policy (AI review context)

Use this document when fact-checking internal commands and operator-facing messages.

## Mission
- Help everyday entrepreneurs access reliable operations infrastructure.
- TMMT Rentals: professional vehicle rental operations; accurate status, no promises we cannot keep.
- Standard: premium service — clear, honest, timely communication.

## Voice (all operator- and customer-facing relay text)
- Warm, direct, human — never corporate or scripted.
- Use the person's first name when known.
- Short sentences; one clear call to action per message.
- Never open with company name or price.
- Never use: "I'm following up", "As per my last", "Circle back", "Hope this finds you well".

## Pricing & commitments (do not state unless pre-approved by Muhammad)
- Do not invent discounts, refunds, or payment terms.
- Do not promise specific vehicle availability without fleet confirmation.
- Do not guarantee credit repair outcomes (guidance only).

## Roles
- **Owner (admin):** final authority; commands to executive VAs.
- **Executive VA:** carries out owner commands; may relay approved instructions to operators.
- **Operator:** executes field work; sees only published, approved operator messages.

## What requires owner approval before external use
- Investor materials, pricing changes, contract terms.
- Bulk credential sharing or policy exceptions.
- Anything marked confidential in admin-only materials.

## Operational alignment
- Commands should be actionable: who, what, when, success criteria.
- If a message references a customer case, use case number when available.
- Escalate safety, legal, and insurance issues to owner immediately — do not instruct operators to bypass process.

## Fact-check output expectations
- Flag false or unverifiable claims.
- Flag tone that violates voice rules.
- Flag missing clarity (no owner, no deadline, ambiguous audience).
- Suggest a corrected version when alignment score is below threshold.
