# Customer Message Template Library

Use these templates for routine car rental communication. Fill all bracketed fields before sending.

## Booking Confirmation

Trigger: Immediately after booking is made.

```text
Hi [NAME], your reservation is confirmed.

Pick-up: [DATE] at [TIME]
Location: [ADDRESS]
Vehicle: [CAR YEAR/MAKE/MODEL]

Please have the following ready at pick-up:
- Valid driver's license
- Matching credit/debit card
- Proof of insurance, if required

Reply to this message with any questions.
We look forward to serving you.

[YOUR BUSINESS NAME]
```

## Pick-Up Reminder

Trigger: 24 hours before pick-up.

```text
Hi [NAME], just a reminder your rental is tomorrow.

[DATE] at [TIME]
[ADDRESS]
[VEHICLE]

Please arrive on time. If anything changes, contact us ASAP at [PHONE NUMBER].

See you tomorrow.

[YOUR BUSINESS NAME]
```

## Day-Of Pick-Up Message

Trigger: Morning of pick-up, send by 8:00 AM.

```text
Good morning [NAME], today's the day.

Your [VEHICLE] is ready for you.
Address: [ADDRESS]
Pick-up time: [TIME]

Running late? Text us at [PHONE].
We hold reservations for [HOLD TIME].

[YOUR BUSINESS NAME]
```

## Vehicle Handoff Checklist Message

Trigger: At pick-up.

```text
Before you drive off, let's confirm everything:

Vehicle condition review:
We will walk the car together and note any pre-existing damage on your rental agreement.

You are responsible for:
- Returning with the same fuel level
- No new damage
- Returning by [RETURN DATE] at [TIME]
- [ANY OTHER POLICIES]

Any questions before you go? Please ask now.

[YOUR BUSINESS NAME]
```

## Return Reminder

Trigger: 24 hours before return due.

```text
Hi [NAME], just a reminder your rental is due back tomorrow.

Return by: [DATE] at [TIME]
Drop-off: [ADDRESS]

Need to extend? Contact us today. Extensions are based on availability.

[PHONE / CONTACT METHOD]

[YOUR BUSINESS NAME]
```

## Late Return Warning

Trigger: 30 minutes after missed return time.

```text
Hi [NAME], we noticed your rental has not been returned yet.

Your return was due: [DATE] at [TIME].

Please contact us immediately at [PHONE NUMBER].

Late fees may apply per your rental agreement.
We need to hear from you within [RESPONSE WINDOW].

[YOUR BUSINESS NAME]
```

## Post-Rental Thank You and Review Request

Trigger: Within 1 hour of return confirmation.

```text
Hi [NAME], thanks for renting with us.

We hope you enjoyed your [VEHICLE].

If you had a great experience, a review helps us a lot:
[REVIEW LINK]

Need a vehicle again? Book here:
[BOOKING LINK]

Thanks again. We appreciate your business.

[YOUR BUSINESS NAME]
```

## Damage Report Notification

Trigger: When damage is found at return. Owner review required before sending.

```text
Hi [NAME], during our post-rental inspection we identified damage that was not present at pick-up.

Damage noted: [DESCRIPTION]
Photos have been documented.

Per your rental agreement, you are responsible for damages incurred during your rental period.

Our team will be in touch within [TIMEFRAME] regarding next steps.

[YOUR BUSINESS NAME]
```

## Payment Due Reminder

Trigger: 3 days before due date.

```text
Hi [NAME], friendly reminder your rental payment of $[AMOUNT] is due on [DATE].

You can pay via [PAYMENT METHOD / LINK].

Reply if you need a payment plan before the due date.

[YOUR BUSINESS NAME]
```

## Payment Overdue Notice

Trigger: Day after due date (owner-approved; legal review for your state).

```text
Hi [NAME], our records show $[AMOUNT] was due on [DATE] and is now overdue.

Please contact us today at [PHONE] to pay or set up a plan.

Unpaid balances may affect your rental status per your agreement.

[YOUR BUSINESS NAME]
```

## Quick Replies

### Where do I pick up the car?

```text
Hi [NAME], pick-up is at:
[FULL ADDRESS]

Your time: [TIME]
Parking: [INSTRUCTIONS]

See you then.
```

### Can I return it early?

```text
Hi [NAME], yes, early returns are fine.

Please let us know your new return time so we can be ready.

Refunds for early returns: [YOUR POLICY]
```

### Can I extend my rental?

```text
Hi [NAME], extensions depend on availability.

Your current return is [DATE/TIME].
Let me check and get back to you within [RESPONSE TIME].

What dates do you need?
```

### What is your address?

```text
Hi [NAME], we are located at:
[FULL ADDRESS]

[PARKING/ENTRY INSTRUCTIONS]

Let us know if you need anything else.
```

### Is the car available on [DATE]?

```text
Hi [NAME], let me check availability for [DATE].
I will confirm within [RESPONSE TIME].

[YOUR BUSINESS NAME]
```
